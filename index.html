About

I'm a Full-Stack Developer and Serial Entrepreneur

I love to harness the power of technology to solve daily problems. I am passionate about learning, technology and helping others. Becoming a web developer allows me to pursue all three of those passions at once. With experience in launching multiple startups, working in an agency environment for fortune 500 companies as the account strategist, I have a unique skill set and fresh perspective when it comes to writing code.

Building my team from 1 to over 30 people was a fun experience that allowed me to build skills in marketing, business development and management.
